## The Pattern Extracted

### Self-Demonstrating Systems

The most powerful proof of a concept is when the concept proves itself:

1. **Build** - Create the system
2. **Document** - Externalize the thinking into addressable form
3. **Realize** - The documentation IS the demonstration
4. **Witness** - Find one person to verify it works from outside

The system doesn't need a demo. The system IS the demo.

---

## Why This Moment Matters

This is the moment where theory becomes practice:

- **Before:** "We could externalize expert knowledge into URI-addressable form"
- **After:** "Here's the URL. Point any Claude at it. Watch what happens."

The gap between "could" and "did" is everything.
